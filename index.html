About Me

Hi, I'm Himmat! I'm a full-stack software engineer who thrives on solving complex problems and designing thoughtful, user-centered solutions. My experience spans technical and creative projects, with a focus on secure, scalable application design and AI-driven innovation. Most recently, I worked as a Software Engineer at Alkami Technology, where I developed and optimized over 20 UI screens and built a Groovy-based service that helped protect over 18.5 million users from fraud.

I'm especially fascinated by the intersection of AI and software architecture and am actively exploring how AI can be harnessed to make software more adaptable and impactful. My path into software engineering is enriched by my background in education—having taught and worked at an international boarding school for six years—and my experiences living abroad for 13 years in India and Russia. These experiences have deepened my appreciation for communication, adaptability, and cultural connections.

When it comes to location, I'm flexible and open to exciting opportunities—whether remote or in cities where I can grow professionally and personally. I'm passionate about working with teams that value innovation, creativity, and making a meaningful impact. If that sounds like your team, let's connect!
